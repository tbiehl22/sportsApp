The Portland Trail Blazers were founded by Harry Glickman who was interested in having an NBA team in his hometown of Portland, Oregon. The Blazers played their first season in 1970-71. They won their first and only NBA Championship in 1977 by defeating the Philadelphia 76ers 4 games to 2. The Blazers wouldn't appear in the NBA Finals again until the 1989-90 season where they would fall to the Detroit Pistons 4-1. A few laters they would take on the Chicago Bulls in the 1992 NBA Finals and lose 4-2. To this date they have not appeared in another NBA Finals.
